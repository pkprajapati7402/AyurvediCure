    • Vata Dosha: Use warm bhringraj oil or sesame oil.
    • Pitta Dosha: Use cooling oils like amla oil or coconut oil.
    • Kapha Dosha: Use stimulating oils like rosemary oil mixed with a base oil.

    Ayurvedic herbal hair masks nourish the scalp, condition the hair, and prevent hair fall. Here are a few DIY hair mask recipes:

    • For Dandruff: Mix 2 tablespoons of neem powder, 1 tablespoon of yogurt, and a few drops of tea tree oil. Apply to your scalp, leave for 30 minutes, and rinse.
    • For Conditioning: Mix 1 mashed banana, 1 tablespoon of honey, and a few drops of almond oil. Apply to your hair, leave for 30 minutes, and rinse.
